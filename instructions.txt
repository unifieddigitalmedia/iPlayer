INSTRUCTIONS

This repository includes a HTTP API that provides BBC iPlayer with lists of programmes sorted alphabetically.

Each of the listing results include:

- a list of programme titles and images
- the ability to paginate if the letter has more than 20 results
- navigation to other letters

GET STARTED

Simply follow the link https://machel-bbc-iplayer-app.herokuapp.com/ to get started. 

To the left of your screen you will see the letters of the alphabet listed vertically. Click on a letter of the alphabet  to 
list programmes those programme whose titles begins with your choosen letter.

Once your results have been processed you will be able to scroll a list of programmes generated by the HTTP API. At the 
bottom of the screen you will see controls that will allow you to move to the next page of results for listings that generate
more than 20 programmes.
